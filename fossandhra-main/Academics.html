Academics

Academics at Adarsh English Medium School

At Adarsh English Medium School, we focus on building a strong academic foundation that ensures success in education and beyond. Our curriculum combines rigorous academics with opportunities for personal growth, offering a well-rounded education for every student.

Curriculum

At Adarsh English Medium School, our curriculum nurtures a love for learning while meeting educational standards. We use national textbooks for Nursery to 5th grade, focusing on core subjects and play-based learning in kindergarten. For 6th to 10th grade, we follow the state syllabus, ensuring in-depth understanding and preparation for state board exams.

IIT Coaching

At Adarsh English Medium School, we offer specialized IIT coaching from Class VI to help students excel in competitive exams. Regular IIT tests assess progress, and top performers receive certificates to inspire excellence.

Assessment and Examination

We use a structured assessment system at Adarsh English Medium School to track student progress. Slip tests follow each lesson, with four formative exams and three summative exams throughout the year. Parents receive results via SMS and detailed report sheets to ensure timely support for academic development.

Micro-Level Day-Wise Planning

At Adarsh English Medium School, our micro-level day-wise planning maximizes learning outcomes. By breaking down the curriculum into daily objectives, we focus on each student's needs, helping them reach their full potential.
